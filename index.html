Before you start
Finding your people
What is a Circle?
A Circle is the small group chat that forms around an event. It stays open after the event ends — so the friendships keep going. Only attendees and the host can see inside.
Why can't I message someone directly?
IncircleMe conversations live inside Circles — the group that shared an event with you. This keeps things grounded in context. If you both opt into direct chats (Profile → Open to DMs), you can.
How do you verify hosts?
Every host passes ID verification (Stripe Identity), a welcome interview, and a first-event review check. The Trusted tier appears on their passport once they've hosted 3 events with a 4.5+ rating.
When you book
Bookings & payments
How do I cancel a booking?
Go to Bookings → tap the event → Cancel. Full refund if you cancel more than 48h before. 50% refund 24–48h before. No refund after that — but hosts sometimes make exceptions. Ask them in the Circle.
Which cards do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay. All payments are processed through Stripe. We never store your full card number.
When am I charged?
Immediately when you confirm your booking. For Rituals (6-week recurring), we charge the first session and then one session ahead each week. You can stop anytime.
What if the event is cancelled by the host?
Automatic full refund to your original payment method within 5 business days. You'll also get a €5 credit toward your next booking — on us.
After the event
Keeping the Circle going
Can I leave a Circle?
Yes, any time. Long-press the Circle in Chats → Leave. No one is notified.
How does the review flow work?
You'll get a soft prompt the day after. A short star rating + one-line public note is enough. A private note to the host is optional — they see it, we don't publish it.
What's a Memory Capsule?
A small keepsake of the event — the group photo, a few quotes from the Circle, who came. It lands in your archive a week later. You can share it, keep it, or mute it.
Safety & trust
If something feels off
How do I report someone?
Tap the three-dot menu on their profile → Report. We review every report within 24h. Your name is never shared with the person reported.
Tips for meeting new people safely
Share your event plans with a friend. Pick public venues for first meetings. Trust your gut — leaving is always the right call. If you feel unsafe, tap Safety in the Circle chat.
Is my data private?
Yes. We sell nothing to third parties. Your messages are encrypted at rest. You can download or delete your data any time (Settings → Privacy).
Money & refunds
Hosting, tiers & payouts
How do I become a host?
Profile → Become a host. Tell us about you, verify ID, connect a bank via Stripe Connect. Approval within 3–5 business days. No subscription required to start.
What are the tiers and fees?
Basic — free, 5% fee. Pro — €20/mo, 3% fee, analytics + priority support. Premium — €60/mo, 0% fee, boosts + dedicated support. Upgrade anytime.
When do I get paid?
Payouts happen 24h after the event ends, to give attendees time to flag issues. Transferred via Stripe to your bank — usually arrives in 1–3 business days.
How do I handle taxes?
You're an independent host, so you're responsible for your own tax reporting. We generate an annual summary of your earnings (Creator dashboard → Payouts → Download). In Spain this is typically declared as IRPF income.
Do I need insurance?
IncircleMe provides basic liability coverage for events on the platform (up to €1M per event). For high-risk activities, we recommend additional coverage — we can introduce you to partners.
Something broke
The app is broken / a feature doesn't work
Email help@incircleme.com with what happened and a screenshot if possible. We reply within 24h on weekdays, 48h on weekends.
An event was not as described
Write it in your review (the host sees it privately by default) and tap Report on the event if it breaks our guidelines. We investigate within 24h and can issue refunds case by case.
I need my account deleted
Settings → Privacy & Safety → Delete account. All data is removed within 30 days per GDPR. You can download your data first (same screen).
Still stuck?
Write to a real person at help@incircleme.com. We read every message. Answers within 24h on weekdays, 48h on weekends.
