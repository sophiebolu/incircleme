The Problem

The Intimate Experience Economy is Broken

Creators and attendees are stuck with tools built for mass events, not meaningful local connections.

😤

Creators Can't Monetise

Instagram followers don't convert to paying students. WhatsApp + spreadsheets for booking. Manual Bizum payments. 4–6 hours/week wasted on admin.

⚠ 80% time lost to admin
🔍

Discovery is Fragmented

No single platform for finding 8–15 person local experiences. Attendees check Instagram, Meetup, Eventbrite separately — most events found too late.

⚠ ~20% no-show rate
🏢

Big Platforms Don't Fit

Eventbrite and Meetup optimise for 500+ person conferences, not 10-person yoga classes or intimate dinner experiences. No verification. No trust.

⚠ Zero trust layer
The Solution

One App for Creators & Attendees

IncircleMe handles discovery, booking, payments, messaging, and trust — so creators can focus on creating.

🗺️

Hyperlocal Discovery

Interactive map + feed filtered by interest, distance (0.5–10km), and availability.

Questions Investors Ask

Barcelona has 15,000–20,000 active independent creators, 95%+ smartphone penetration, a thriving creator economy culture, and strong post-COVID demand for intimate local experiences. The perfect proving ground before Madrid, Valencia, and other European cities.

Creators receive payments via Stripe Connect with T+2 payouts directly to their bank account. Pro and Elite tier creators pay 0% transaction fees. Basic tier creators pay 8%.

Trust. Every creator is manually verified with ID. Reviews are transparent. Once creators build booking history, reviews, and followers on IncircleMe, switching costs are high. Network effects compound as more attendees discover more creators.

The European local experiences market exceeds €45B annually. With 250K+ independent creators in Spain and growing creator economy tailwinds, IncircleMe targets a €500M+ serviceable market in Western Europe within 5 years.
